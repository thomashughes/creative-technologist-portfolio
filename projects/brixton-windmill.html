Overview

Brixton Windmill is one of only two surviving windmills in London, and this project brings it to life digitally. Using aerial Gaussian splat capture, visitors can explore the windmill from above before zooming down to street level where 360¬∞ photography takes over.

The experience seamlessly transitions between aerial and ground perspectives, with informational hotspots pulling live data from the windmill's official website to ensure content stays current.
