Overview

VisitLichfield is a full-featured tourist application designed to enhance the visitor experience in Lichfield, a historic cathedral city in Staffordshire, England. The app serves multiple user types through three distinct dashboards, each tailored to specific needs.

From tourists exploring the city's medieval streets to administrators managing tour content, the platform provides an intuitive interface backed by robust data management and real-time mapping capabilities.
