Overview

This e-commerce prototype demonstrates how 3D product visualization can enhance online shopping. The shoe was captured using photogrammetry and placed in an interactive viewer where users can click arrow hotspots to snap to specific angles highlighting different features.

Each camera position is paired with an information panel showing details about that part of the shoe ‚Äî the sole, tongue, materials, and other unique selling points. Users retain full control to explore freely after any snap.
