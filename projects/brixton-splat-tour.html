BRIXTON SPLAT TOUR

A walking tour through Brixton's vibrant streets, powered by Gaussian splat technology for an immersive, volumetric experience.

ROLE Creator
YEAR 2025
TYPE Walking Tour

Overview

The Brixton Splat Tour takes visitors on a virtual walking tour through one of London's most culturally rich neighborhoods. Unlike traditional street view, Gaussian splat technology creates volumetric 3D scenes that can be explored from any angle.

Each stop on the tour captures a moment in Brixton's vibrant streetscape, from the iconic market to historic buildings and street art.
